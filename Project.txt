SPARK Project

Theme: Texas Sports Website that displays info, scores, and standings information for
 Texas pro sports teams like, the Cowboys, Texans, Spurs, Rockets, Mavs, Astros and Rangers.

Use cases:
1. Allows users to check up-to-date standings with Texas teams
2. Allows users to see the coaching staff, and roster of Texas teams
3. Allows the user to see Team schedules
4. Users can sign up for monthly newsletter
5. Allows users to see team pictures of current seasons
6. Allows the use of seeing up to date team news
7. The user can look at the website observing the latest statistics for current seasons.
